## ğŸ’¾ Dataset Details

The underlying data (`Healthcare.xlsx`) includes the following key attributes:

- **Date / Year**: Transaction dates for time-series analysis.
- **Region**: Location of the healthcare facility.
- **Department**: The medical department handling the patient.
- **Treatment_Type**: The specific category of care provided.
- **Metrics**:
    - `Number_of_Patients`: Volume of patients treated.
    - `Treatment_Cost`: Cost to the patient/provider.
    - `Total_Revenue`: Gross income generated.
    - `Expense`: Operational costs.
    - `Profit`: Net income (`Total_Revenue - Expense`).
- **Additional Info**: `Currency`, `Payment_Method`.
